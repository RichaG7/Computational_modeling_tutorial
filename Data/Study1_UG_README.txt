Study1_UG README

Sub: refers to unique subject number
Role: participant either played UG as Player B (self) or Player C (other) 
Trial: trial of the UG game, ranges from 1 to 20 
Unfairness: amount of money Player A kept for themselves out of 100 (cents)
Choice: accept (0) or reject (1) 
Choice_RT: reaction time of choice in seconds
